Test QSR Manual: Equipment Cleaning Procedures

Fryer Cleaning:
1. Turn off fryer and wait for oil to cool
2. Drain oil completely  
3. Remove and clean fryer basket
4. Wipe down interior with non-abrasive cloth
5. Refill with fresh oil

Grill Cleaning:
1. Allow grill to cool down
2. Remove cooking grates
3. Clean grates with warm soapy water
4. Scrape grill surface clean
5. Apply light coat of cooking oil
